  - FLR-191–FLR-230 — fresh fruit, preserves, fragrant peel, and seed pits for oil.

- Steppe grains & hardy cereals (FLR-326–FLR-350 — wind-tolerant heads for milling and straw.)
- Jungle vines & canopy climbers (FLR-351–FLR-375 — hanging fruit, ropey stems, and shade greens.)
- River reeds & delta rushes (FLR-376–FLR-400 — buoyant reeds, cores, and sweet shoots.)
- Orchard understory shrubs (FLR-401–FLR-425 — hedge berries, spice pods, and leaf mulch.)
- Medicinal roots & tonics (FLR-426–FLR-450 — potent taproots and tubers for decoctions.)
- Luminescent fungi & cave blooms (FLR-451–FLR-475 — glowcaps, spore fans, and mycelium mats.)
- Sky terrace exotics (FLR-476–FLR-500 — terrace-tended petals, experimental grafts, and spice trials.)
